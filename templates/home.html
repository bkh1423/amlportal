AML Risk & Compliance Assessment

Welcome to the AML Risk Assessment Tool.
This system helps you evaluate your compliance and AML risk levels efficiently and accurately.
You’ll go through three main sections:

1. Business Profile – Define your business type and structure.
2. Compliance Stack – Describe your current AML tools and systems.
3. Risk Questions – Answer targeted questions to assess your risk exposure.

After completion, your overall risk level and recommendations will appear automatically.
